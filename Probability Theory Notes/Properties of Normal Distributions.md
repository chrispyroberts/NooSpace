Notation: $X\sim N(\mu, \sigma^{2})$
Support: $\mathbb{R}$
Parameters: $\mu:$ Mean,  $\sigma^{2}:$ Variance
PDF: 
$$f_{X}(x)=\frac{1}{\sqrt{2\pi \sigma^{2}}}\exp\bigg(-\frac{(x-\mu)^{2}}{2\sigma^{2}}\bigg), x\in\mathbb{R}$$Expected Value: $\mathbb{E}[X]= \mu$
Variance: $\text{Var}[X]= \sigma^{2}$
